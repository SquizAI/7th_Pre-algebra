# 8th Grade Pre-Algebra Curriculum Documentation

## Overview

This directory contains curriculum planning files for **8th Grade Pre-Algebra** at Centner Academy Miami for the 2025-2026 school year.

**Course Information:**
- Course Code: 1205070 - M/J Grade 8 Pre-Algebra
- Schedule: B-day schedule (90-minute blocks, every other day)
- School: Centner Academy Miami
- Academic Year: 2025-2026

## Important Files

### Academic Calendar
- `24 - 25 MS_HS Academic Calendar Expanded - 25-26 A_B Schedule 2.csv` - School calendar with A/B day schedule

### Standards & Curriculum
- `8th_BEST_math_standards_2025_PRE_ALGEBRA.json` - Florida BEST Standards for 8th Grade Pre-Algebra (Course 1205070)

### Quarterly Lesson Plans
- `Q1_8th_grade_detailed_lessons.json` - Quarter 1 lesson plans and activities
- `Q2_8th_grade_detailed_lessons.json` - Quarter 2 lesson plans and activities
- `Q3_8th_grade_detailed_lessons.json` - Quarter 3 lesson plans and activities
- `Q4_8th_grade_detailed_lessons.json` - Quarter 4 lesson plans and activities

### Lesson Calendar Mapping
- `lesson_calendar_B_days_2025-2026.json` - Maps lessons to specific B-day dates throughout the school year
