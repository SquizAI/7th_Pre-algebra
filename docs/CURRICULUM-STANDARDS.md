## ğŸ“ˆ Pacing Guide

### Standard Pacing (75-minute periods)
- **Introduction**: 1 period (tutorial + first levels)
- **Practice**: 2-3 periods (guided and independent)
- **Mastery**: 1 period (assessment + review)
- **Total per standard**: 4-5 periods (1-1.5 weeks)

### Flexible Pacing Options
- **Accelerated** (advanced students): 2-3 periods per standard
- **Standard**: 4-5 periods per standard
- **Extended** (struggling students): 6-8 periods with additional practice

---

## ğŸ“ Mastery Criteria

Students demonstrate mastery when they can:

1. **Solve independently**: 80%+ accuracy on 5+ problems
2. **Explain steps**: Articulate why each step is taken
3. **Apply to new situations**: Transfer skills to word problems
4. **Identify errors**: Recognize and correct mistakes
5. **Teach others**: Help peers understand the process
